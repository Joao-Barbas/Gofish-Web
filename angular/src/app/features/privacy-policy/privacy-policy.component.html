GOFISH's Privacy Policy

Effective: 12 April 2026

Last Updated: 12 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GOFISH collects, uses, and protects information when users access or use the platform.

By creating an account or using the service, users agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

  1. Information We Collect
  2. How We Use the Information
  3. Third-Party Services
  4. Data Retention
  5. User Rights
  6. Security Measures
  7. Children's Privacy
  8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When users create an account or interact with the platform, GOFISH may collect the following information:

Account Information

  • Username
  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted/hashed form)

User Content

  • Pins created on the map
  • Geographic location of pins
  • Images uploaded with pins
  • Information related to fishing catches or environmental observations

User Feedback

  • Satisfaction ratings or feedback voluntarily provided by users through in-app surveys.

2. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used only for operating and maintaining the platform.

This includes:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Displaying pins and user-generated content within the platform
  • Allowing interaction between users and the map features
  • Maintaining the functionality and integrity of the service
  • Collecting voluntary user satisfaction feedback to improve the platform

3. Third-Party Services

GOFISH relies on third-party services to provide certain functionalities.

These services include:

  • Mapbox for map visualization and geolocation features
  • Google for optional authentication and account sign-in
  • Microsoft cloud infrastructure through Azure hosting services

These providers may process certain technical data required to deliver their services and are subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

User information is stored for as long as the account remains active.

If a user deletes their account, associated personal data and content are removed from the platform, except where retention is required for technical or legal reasons.

5. User Rights

Users have control over their personal information within the platform.

Users may:

  • View their profile information
  • Update or correct their personal data
  • Delete their account and associated data

These controls are available directly within the application.

6. Security Measures

GOFISH implements measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse.

These measures include:

  • Secure password hashing for stored credentials
  • Encrypted communication through HTTPS connections
  • Optional Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for additional account security

Authentication applications such as Google Authenticator may be used for this purpose.

7. Children's Privacy

The GOFISH platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13.

By creating an account, users confirm that they meet this minimum age requirement.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

GOFISH may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of the platform after changes have been published indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
